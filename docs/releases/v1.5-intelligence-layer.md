This release introduces the complete Intelligence Layer of the NeoLegal Production Platform.



The platform no longer only produces legal knowledge cards.

\### 205.6 Logger Intelligence



Analyzes platform logs.

\### 205.7 Platform Stability Index



Produces an overall platform stability score.



\---



\### 205.8 Health Trend Engine



Measures health score trends.



Evaluates platform evolution.

\### 205.9 Forecast Engine



Estimates future production progress.



Provides production forecasting.
